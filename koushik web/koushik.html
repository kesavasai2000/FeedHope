How FeedHope Works

1. Report Excess Food

Restaurants or marriage halls notify us about their excess food via app or call

2. Hygienic Collection

Our trained volunteers collect food in food-grade containers within 60 minutes

3. Delivery to Orphanages

Food is delivered to verified orphanages while still fresh and nutritious

Our Food Safety Promise

  • All food collected within 2 hours of preparation
  • Temperature-controlled transportation
  • Hygienic packaging with food-grade materials
  • Regular quality checks and volunteer training
